Opening Swift Playgrounds on Mac:

  1. Click on the Finder icon in the Dock (it looks like a blue smiling face).
  2. In the Finder window that appears, click on Applications in the sidebar on the left.
  3. Scroll through the list of applications to find Swift Playgrounds. It has a white icon with an orange bird.
  4. Double-click the Swift Playgrounds icon to open it.
  5. If it's your first time opening it, you might see a welcome screen. You can close this or, if prompted, choose to "Start with an App" or look for a "New Project" / "New App" option. For the bootcamp, we'll typically start a new App project.
